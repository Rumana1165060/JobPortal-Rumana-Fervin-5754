Job description

We are looking for ML Engineers for building AI/ML products for extracting the insights from the Bigdata and for optimizing the mortgage process engineering. The ideal candidate will be part of the complete life cycle of AI/ML projects that includes data collection, curation, EDA, model training, integration, productionization, monitoring, and so on, and should be a good team player and passionate about artificial intelligence and stay up-to-date with the latest developments in the field.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Understanding business objectives and developing models to achieve them, along with metrics to track their progress.
  • Analysing large and complex datasets to extract insights and decide on the appropriate technique.
  • Exploratory Data Analysis, Visualization, and Analysing the ML algorithms that could be used to solve a given problem and ranking them by their success probability.
  • Supervising the data acquisition process if more data is needed. Finding available datasets online that could be used for training.
  • Defining validation strategies, pre-processing, or feature engineering to be done on a given dataset, and defining data augmentation pipelines, if required.
  • Training models and tuning their hyperparameters; Analysing the errors of the model and designing strategies to overcome them; Deploying models to production.
  • Demonstrate the end-to-end understanding of applications (including, but not limited to, the machine learning algorithms) being built.
  • Managing available resources such as hardware, data, and personnel so that objectives are met.
  • Communicate and explain complex processes to people who are not programming experts.
  • Research and implement best practices to improve the existing machine learning infrastructure.
