Job description

  • Should have well knowledge on ICD 10 cm coding
  • The candidate should be able to read, understand the operative reports and append the CPT procedures codes

ED-Professional Coding:

  • Should have good knowledge in Emergency department coding ( ER coding)
  • Must have a min of 4 years and of ED Professional coding experience
  • Certified Professional Coder (CPC) credential.
  • Good communication and responsiveness
